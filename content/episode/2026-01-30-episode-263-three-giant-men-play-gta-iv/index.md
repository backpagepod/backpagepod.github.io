## Pain in the arse missions
- Pegorino's Pride
- Tunnel of Death
- Truck Hustle
- Any time you have to tail a vehicle of any kind.
- Any mission that asks you to pursue a character and where you can reach that character clearly by killing them with your gun but the game has protected them.
- Any mission where you have to go into a very tight physical space.
